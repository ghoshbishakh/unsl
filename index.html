Ubiquitous Networked Systems Lab

We’re constantly trying to push the boundaries of sensing and systems research. Come along if you share the same ambition.

Research Focus

3p
Computer Human Interaction

We work in the intersection of theoretical computer science, machine learning, and systems to develop platforms and applications for CHI, specifically on assistive systems, and man-machine interfacing.

payments
Sensing

Our sensing research focuses on developing innovative applications over smartphones, wearables, fixed sensing infrastructure, etc. We explore sensing modalities including locomotive, acoustic, RF, and so on.

apps
Distributed Systems

Our works on distributed systems span over areas like cloud and edge computing, decentralized web and blockchains, geo-distributed resource management, and serverless computing.

public
Networking

Under networking research, we broadly work on Optimizations in Networking Protocols, Quality of Experience (QoE), Network Application Integration, and System supports for Networking.
